Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about blood donation and our services.

How often can I donate blood?

You can donate whole blood every 56 days (8 weeks). For platelets, you can donate every 7 days up to 24 times a year.

What are the eligibility requirements?

You must be at least 17 years old, weigh at least 110 pounds, and be in good general health. Additional requirements may apply.

How long does the donation process take?

The entire process takes about 1 hour, including registration, health screening, and the actual donation which takes 8-10 minutes.

Is blood donation safe?

Yes, blood donation is very safe. All equipment is sterile and used only once. You cannot get any disease from donating blood.
