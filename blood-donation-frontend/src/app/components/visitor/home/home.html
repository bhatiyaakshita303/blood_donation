How it works

Donating blood is safe, simple, and saves lives. Here is what to expect.

1. Registration

Sign up, complete a brief health questionnaire, and schedule your donation.

2. Health Check

A quick mini-physical checking your temperature, blood pressure, and hemoglobin.

3. Donation

The actual donation takes about 8-10 minutes. Relax while you save lives.
